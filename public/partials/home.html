Who are we?

Yellow Bag of Humanity is an organization comprised of a group of people that understand and support those who are enduring their journey through cancer.

What is our purpose?

Our purpose is to provide an accessible, simple to navigate, outlet for friends or family to uplift and gift a care package for their loved one who has been diagnosed with cancer.
