Who is Yellow Bag of Humanity?

We are providing totes of support, offering comfort, care, and encouragement to those who are undergoing treatment.

What is our purpose?

Our purpose is to provide an accessible, simple to navigate, outlet for friends, family, or anonymous supporters to uplift, gift a care package for their loved one, or make a donation to someone who has been diagnosed with cancer.
